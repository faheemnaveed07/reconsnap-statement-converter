# ASO Strategy

## Store Title

Recommended title:

`ReconSnap Statement Converter`

Reason:

- Keeps the ReconSnap brand.
- Includes high-intent "Statement Converter" keyword.
- Fits within the 30-character store title limit.

## Short Description

Google Play short description:

`Convert bank PDFs to Excel, CSV, QBO and Xero with balance checks.`

## Subtitle

Apple subtitle:

`Bank PDF to Excel and CSV`

## Keyword Targets

Primary:

- bank statement converter
- bank statement to excel
- PDF bank statement converter
- bank statement to CSV

Secondary:

- QuickBooks bank statement
- Xero bank statement
- PDF to Excel finance
- statement reconciliation
- bookkeeping CSV

## Screenshot Captions

1. Bank Statement to Excel
2. Upload PDF or Scanned Statement
3. Review and Fix Extracted Rows
4. Balance Check Before Export
5. Built for Accountants and SMEs

## Positioning Rules

- Do not claim "1000+ banks" without evidence.
- Do not claim "99% accuracy" until measured.
- Lead with validation and accountant trust.
- Use bank-specific landing pages later for SEO.
- Keep privacy clear: no bank credentials, temporary uploads only when needed.
